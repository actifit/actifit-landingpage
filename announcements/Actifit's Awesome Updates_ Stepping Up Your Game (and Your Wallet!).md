# Actifit's Awesome Updates: Stepping Up Your Game (and Your Wallet!)

Alright Actifitters! Get ready for some fantastic updates that are rolling out faster than your daily steps! Our dev team has been hard at work, sweating it out (just like you!) to bring you a smoother, snappier, and all-around more enjoyable Actifit experience in our latest version!

![image.png](https://usermedia.actifit.io/MLJHUF9BPCVNUQCW0EAVKXVG7ZR0W)

Here's a peek at what's fresh from the oven:

**🎥 Video Vibes Just Got Better: 3speak Embeds are SO Smooth Now!**
Ever tried to catch up on a 3speak video and it was a bit... glitchy? Well, say goodbye to those woes! We've given our 3speak video embeds a shiny new update, making them load faster, look better, and generally behave like the well-behaved videos they were always meant to be. Get ready for an uninterrupted viewing pleasure! (Special thanks to 3speak team and devs for their work on this - @meno, @eddiespino, @sagarkothari88 to name a few)  

**💰 Wallet Wonders: Your Funds, Our Focus!**
We know your Actifit wallet is important to you, and it's super important to us too! We've ironed out a few pesky wrinkles to ensure your transactions are as reliable as your daily activity.
*   **JSON Jitters? Gone!** We fixed an underlying technical hiccup that ensures your wallet data is always parsed perfectly. No more sneaky errors trying to mess with your numbers!
*   **Powerdown Perfection!** For those of you powering down, we've fine-tuned the calculations to make sure the amounts are spot-on. Every hive-toshi accounted for!
*   **Balance Bliss!** Your available balance indicator and overall wallet balances will now update more reliably and consistently. No more head-scratching wondering if your funds are where they should be!
*   **Speedy Syncing!** We've also boosted the performance of your balance updates, so your wallet reflects your efforts quicker than ever before.

**✨ UI/UX & Content Improvements!**
*   **Cleaner Comments, Better Experience:** We're now hiding low-reputation comments by default to ensure a more positive and focused discussion environment. You can still choose to view them if you wish!
*   **Smarter Landing Page, Warmer Welcome:** Our landing page has received a significant facelift! We've added new, highlighted action buttons to guide you seamlessly, and there's a special bonus announcement just for our new users to kickstart their Actifit journey.
*   **Single Post View - Edit & Delete:** You can now conveniently edit or delete your own posts directly from the single post view, giving you more control over your content.
*   **Keychain Comment Deletion Fix:** We've resolved an issue with keychain comment deletion, ensuring you can manage your comments smoothly.

**✨ Eye-Catching Enhancements: Prize Details Pop!**
We've made a subtle but impactful change: prize details now have enhanced contrast! This means all those awesome rewards you're eyeing will stand out even more, making it easier to see what you're working towards.

**🚀 Exploring New Horizons: Single Post Features!**
While we're keeping some of the magic under wraps for a full reveal soon, we've laid the groundwork for some exciting new features around single posts. Get ready for more ways to interact and engage with content on Actifit!

**⚡️ Under the Hood: A Faster, Smoother Actifit! (Web Version 1.8.3 - Live on Actifit.io!)**
We have also made major infrastructure updates and database query optimizations to ensure a smoother and faster experience across the platform.
*   **Gemini-Powered Translations:** We've fixed and enhanced our translation functionality by leveraging new Gemini models, providing more accurate and natural language translations across the platform.

**🐞 Other Fixes & Improvements!**
*   **Reply Button Visibility:** The reply button visibility has been fixed, making it easier to engage in conversations.
*   **Sidebar User Profile Distortion:** We've ironed out issues causing distortion in the sidebar user profile, ensuring a clean and consistent look.
*   **And So Much More:** These are just a few highlights! We've also pushed numerous other bug fixes, performance enhancements, and minor improvements to make your Actifit experience even better.

We're constantly striving to make Actifit the best it can be, and your feedback helps us get there. Keep moving, keep sharing, and keep an eye out for even more awesomeness heading your way!

Happy Actifitting!
The Actifit Team
